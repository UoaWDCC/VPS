# Getting Started with VPS

VPS is a tool that supports interactive and immersive education through scene based branching scenarios.

The core workflow is quite simple:

## 1. Creating a Scenario

On the home page, navigate to the **Create & Edit** tab and press the big plus button. Then, enter in the name for your scenario and press create. Congrats, you now have a scenario!

## 2. Editing your Scenario

The above step should take you to the editor. You can also get to the editor by pressing any of your scenarios from the **Create & Edit** tab. Visit [Editor](/editor/) to learn the full capabilities of the editor.

## 3. Assigning your scenario

After you've created a scenario, you can assign it to individuals or groups so that they can access and play it. Visit [Assignment](/assignment/) to see how this works.

## 4. Viewing the Dashboard

The app has a dashboard view for scenario authors to view the progress of individuals and groups assigned to a scenario. From here you can view the current and historical game state, and see the paths that players are taking through your scenario. Visit [Dashboard](/dashboard/) to learn how to use it.
